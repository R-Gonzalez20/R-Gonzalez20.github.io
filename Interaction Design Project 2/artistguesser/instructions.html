Name That Artist

Instructions

Welcome to Name That Artist, a musical guessing game! As you may know, many of your favorite musicians use a fake/stage name. In this quiz, you will be guessing what artists' real names are! With each question, you will be shown a legal, government name, along with 3 different musicians. Your job is to click the artist whose name you believe is being shown above. If you are correct, you will be able to move on to the next question. If you are incorrect, you will have to go back and try again until you get the correct answer. Good Luck!
