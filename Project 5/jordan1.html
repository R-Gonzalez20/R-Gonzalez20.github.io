AIR JORDAN 1
THE NIKE AIR JORDAN 1 IS ARGUABLY ONE OF IF NOT THE MOST ICONIC SNEAKER OF ALL TIME. BEING THAT IT IS THE FIRST SHOE IN THE AIR JORDAN SERIES AND THE FIRST SHOE DESIGNED FOR MICHAEL JORDAN TO BE WORN ON COURT IN AN NBA GAME, IT HOLDS SIGNIFICANT VALUE IN SNEAKER AND FASHION CULTURE. IT IS INFAMOUS FOR BEING BANNED BY THE NBA UPON ITS RELEASE DUE TO DRESS CODE VIOLATIONS. THIS NIKE SILHOUETTE HAS REMAINED POPULAR THROUGHOUT ITS LIFESPAN AND HAS BEEN UTILIZED IN A NUMBER OF NOTABLE COLLABORATIONS INCLUDING TRAVIS SCOTT/CACTUS JACK, OFF-WHITE, AND MORE.
