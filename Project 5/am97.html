AIR MAX 97
THE AIR MAX 97 WAS THE FIRST OF THE AIR MAX SHOES TO BE COMPLETELY FOAMLESS IN ITS MAKEUP. IT WAS ALSO THE FIRST SHOE WITH 1 LARGE AIR UNIT THAT CONNECTED FROM THE HEEL TO TOE IN THE SOLE AND ITS OVERALL DESIGN IS BELIEVED TO BE INSPIRED BY JAPAN’S BULLET TRAINS. THE AIR MAX 97 HAS BEEN INCLUDED IN MANY NOTABLE COLLABORATIONS INCLUDING OFF-WHITE AS WELL AS SEAN WOTHERSPOON (PICTURED.) THE AIR MAX 97 WAS BUZZING IN THE MEDIA RECENTLY WHEN LIL NAS X AND MSCHF RELEASED THEIR “SATAN SHOES,” A BOOTLEG VERSION OF THE SHOE THAT HAD WAS RUMED TO BE HUMAN BLOOD INJECTED IN THE SOLES AS WELL AS SATANIC DECOR.
